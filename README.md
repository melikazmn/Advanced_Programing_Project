# Advanced_Programing_Project
Advanced Programing Project using JOptionPane which has a manager who can add professors, students, terms, lessons.
professors can choose what lesson they want to teach and they can add exam for the lesson they have chosen. they can check if students who have taken the exam wants to know why they got such a grade and professors can change their students grade if they want.
students can sign in lesson in each term and they can take the exam of the lessons they have signed in and get their grade and send message to their professor and professor will check the message and change their grades if it is needed.students can see all of their grades too.
